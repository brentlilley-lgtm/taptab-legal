TapTab Legal

Bill splitting where the receipt photo is the interface.

TapTab is a mobile app for splitting restaurant bills. Snap the receipt, tap the items each person ordered, and dispatch payment requests through Venmo, PayPal, Zelle, or Cash App. Money never flows through TapTab.

The promise, in four lines

  • We don't collect or sell your information. No accounts, no analytics beyond crash reports, no advertising.
  • No subscriptions. Ever.
  • No hidden fees. $0.50 per bill split through your phone's app store. That's it.
  • What you're paying for: splitting one bill and dispatching its payment requests.
