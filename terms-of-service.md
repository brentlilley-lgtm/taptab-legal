## 1. What TapTab does

TapTab helps you split a restaurant bill by photographing the receipt, having each person tap the items they ordered, calculating each person's share with proportional tax and tip, and dispatching payment requests to popular payment apps (Venmo, PayPal, Zelle, Cash App).

**TapTab does not transfer funds.** We do not hold, route, or have custody of any money. When you "send a request" through TapTab, we open the relevant payment app on your phone with the recipient and amount prefilled — the actual transaction happens between you, your friends, and those payment providers.
